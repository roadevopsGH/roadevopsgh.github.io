Issue: The ABS light is illuminated on my trailer


Resolution: The ABS light can be a result of many things. In some cases, it is the wheel speed sensor, or the tone ring. Reguardless, it takes a specialty repair and diagnostics to get to the root of the issue. Road Service, Loves, TA, Speedco, these shops do not have the diagnostic software and expertise to get to the root of the issue most of the time. They can easily get the light to turn off, but it will usually come back on just a few miles down the road. We must get this unit to a terminal to get to the root cause of the problem.
